Executive Summary

A facility-level WRAP readiness diagnostic scoring all 12 principles on a 1-to-4 compliance scale. Identify your highest-risk gaps and generate a prioritized action plan — in 10 to 15 minutes, before your auditor does.

Rate your facility honestly against each of the 12 WRAP principles using the 1-to-4 scale shown below. The tool calculates your composite score out of 48, classifies your audit risk tier, and generates a prioritized action list showing exactly which principles need attention first. Your results can be printed or saved as a PDF for your compliance file.

This is a diagnostic, not a substitute for a professional pre-audit assessment. For a full gap review and corrective action plan, schedule a consultation with RC2 Consulting.
