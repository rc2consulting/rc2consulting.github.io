📐 Step-by-Step: How to Calculate Your Rate

1
Calculate Your Cost Floor (Annual Overhead ÷ Billable Hours)

Add: salary/draw + insurance (E&O, GL) + software + marketing + office + admin. Divide by realistic billable hours (800–1,200/yr for solo consultant). This is your MINIMUM breakeven rate. Never price below this.

2
Benchmark the Market

Operations/compliance consultants 10+ yrs: $150–$350/hr. WRAP/government specialists: $200–$450/hr. Fractional COO/DOO: $8,000–$20,000/month. Sources: BLS, Consulting.com, SCORE.org, IBISWorld 2024.

3
Apply the Value Multiplier

Your expertise saves clients $50K–$500K+ in audit failures, fines, and lost contracts. Price at 10–20% of the value you create. A client at risk of losing a $500K government contract should pay $25K–$50K for compliance prep — that is a 10:1 ROI minimum.

4
Add Your Profit Margin

Target 35–55% gross margin on all services. Use the Profit Margin tab to auto-calculate this on every quote. Never quote without checking your margin first.

5
Choose the Right Model for the Client

Government/large manufacturers: Fixed project fee + milestone payments. SMBs new to compliance: Retainer or task-based to reduce sticker shock. Short discovery: Hourly or flat discovery fee ($500–$2,000).

6
Protect Yourself: Scope Definition

Every fixed-fee quote MUST include a written SOW with deliverables, 2-revision limit, out-of-scope items, and a change order clause at your hourly rate. Never start without a signed agreement and 30–50% deposit.

7
Communicate Value, Not Hours

Never justify your price by hours worked. Justify by: risks avoided, certifications gained, contracts won, audits passed. Say "This protects your $2M WRAP contract" — not "This is 40 hours at $200."

🌐 Triple-Horizon Pricing Strategy

Horizon 1 — 0 to 12 Months
Launch Phase

Start at 65–75% of target rates to win first 5–8 clients and build case studies. Accept hourly for early engagements. Rate: $175–$225/hr. Goal: $10K–$20K/month. Offer a Founding Client Rate with testimonial agreement.

Horizon 2 — 1 to 3 Years
Growth Phase

Raise to full market rates. Shift to 60% retainer/project, 40% hourly. Hire junior associate to leverage. Rate: $225–$295/hr. Target: $30K–$70K/month. Specialize in 2–3 high-margin niches: WRAP + Gov + Fractional.

Horizon 3 — 3 to 10 Years
Scale Phase

Position as premium boutique firm. Rate: $300–$450/hr for principal. Productize: courses, toolkits, compliance subscriptions. Target $100K–$300K/month. Raise rates 8–12% annually once established.

⚡ Scope Protection Rules

1
Define Every Deliverable

List exactly what is included. Ambiguity equals scope creep equals lost profit.

2
State What Is NOT Included

Explicitly exclude tasks not in scope. Example: "Travel expenses billed separately at cost."

3
Limit Revisions to 2 Rounds

Additional revisions billed at your standard hourly rate per the change order clause.

4
Change Order Clause Required

Any scope change requires a written change order signed before additional work begins.

5
Milestone Payment Structure

50% at signing, 25% at midpoint delivery, 25% at final delivery. Never deliver final product before final payment.

💡 RC2 Pricing Rules — Engrave These

✅
Always Know Your Margin Before Quoting

Run the Margin Calculator on every engagement. Never quote from your gut. 40% minimum, 45–55% target.

✅
Get 30–50% Deposit Before Starting Work

No exceptions. Non-negotiable for new clients. Filters serious buyers and protects your time.

✅
Price on Value, Not Hours

Frame every quote by what it protects or creates for the client — not by how long it takes you.

✅
Annual Rate Reviews — 5–10% Every Year

Always include escalation clauses in long-term contracts. Your expertise compounds over time.

🚫
Never Start Without a Signed Agreement

Verbal agreements are unenforceable. Always have a written SOW or contract signed before billing a single hour.

🚫
Never Discount More Than 15% Without Compensating Value

Discounting destroys perceived value. Offer payment plans, phased projects, or reduced scope instead of a lower rate.

🚫
Never Work Spec or Free to Show Value

Discovery calls: 30–45 minutes free. Full assessments: always charge. Your expertise is the product.

🚫
Never Omit a Change Order Clause

All scope changes require signed change orders at your standard rate. Document every agreement in writing.
