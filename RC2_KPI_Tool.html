Executive Summary

An interactive KPI framework scoring tool covering 6 organizational performance domains — Financial, Customer, Operations, Compliance, People, and Strategic. Name your KPIs, rate their current maturity and strategic importance, and get a real-time composite score with a prioritized action list identifying where to focus first.

For each of the 6 domains, rate your current KPIs on a 5-point maturity scale (1=Not Measured → 5=Fully Optimized) and their Strategic Importance (1–5). The tool calculates a weighted composite score and identifies the highest-priority gaps — the KPIs where low maturity meets high strategic importance.

You can rename any KPI to match what your organization actually tracks, and add custom KPIs using the + button in each domain. Use Export Summary to create a printable framework for your leadership team or operations advisor.
